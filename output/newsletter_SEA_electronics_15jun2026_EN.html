Global Industry Shifts

China+1 Goes Mainstream as SEA Manufacturing Hub Solidifies

Supply chain diversification is now standard practice. Vietnam, Thailand, and Indonesia are primary beneficiaries as global brands add production capacity outside China. Foxconn’s cumulative investment in Bac Ninh province, Vietnam, has reached $4 billion, creating 130,000 jobs.

→ Downstream SEA impact: Increased EMS/ODM capacity for smartphones and home appliances.
Source: Dimerco, The Asset, Z2Data

SEA Smartphone Market: Volume Down, Value Up in Q1 2026

Omdia reports shipments fell 9% YoY to 21.6M units, but ASP rose 19% to $349. Samsung was the only top-5 brand growing (+4% YoY). OPPO, Xiaomi, TRANSSION, and vivo all declined. Vendors prioritized profitability over volume.

→ Downstream SEA impact: Brands are shifting to higher-margin models; entry-level segment shrinking.
Source: Omdia, AFP, Android Headlines

MLCC Price Hikes Loom as AI Demand Tightens Supply

Yageo signals potential price increases after Murata and Samsung Electro-Mechanics issued price hike notices. AI demand is driving raw material cost increases and tight supply-demand conditions expected to materialize in 2H26.

→ Downstream SEA impact: Higher BOM costs for SEA-based smartphone and appliance OEMs.
Source: Digitimes, X (Jukan)

Core Component Supply Chain Update

PCB/FPC: Thailand and Vietnam Emerge as World-Class PCB Hubs

TLB announced $80.9M rights issue to build a new plant in Vietnam, doubling PCB capacity for AI and data center demand. Panasonic invested $47.11M in a new MEGTRON circuit board material line in China. Vietnam is dominated by Japanese, Korean, and Taiwanese fabricators; Malaysia attracts global players like TTM and AT&S.

📍 Vietnam, Thailand · Status: Expansion

Impact: Increased local PCB supply for SEA-based EMS providers.

Display: Samsung Display to Build $1.8B OLED Plant in Vietnam

Samsung Display’s new module production line in Vietnam will process 10 million units yearly, hedging against trade friction. LG Display is exiting LCD, selling its Guangzhou plant. OLED monitor panel shipments are forecast to hit 5.4M units in 2026, up 60% YoY.

📍 Vietnam · Status: Investment

Impact: Strengthens Vietnam’s role in premium display assembly for smartphones and IT.

Camera Modules: LG Innotek Completes Vietnam Plant Expansion

LG Innotek finished construction of its V3 camera module plant in Hai Phong, Vietnam, as part of a cost-cutting strategy. The plant supports high-precision active alignment and multi-camera module production for global smartphone OEMs.

📍 Vietnam · Status: Operational

Impact: Increases regional capacity for high-end camera modules; benefits Apple supply chain.

Battery: ATL and BYD Expand SEA Footprint

ATL (Amperex Technology) is planning a new battery cell factory in Southeast Asia. BYD is partnering with Kim Long Motor to build a $130M EV battery plant in central Vietnam for commercial vehicles, with potential expansion to passenger cars.

📍 Vietnam, SEA · Status: Planned

Impact: Localizes battery production for consumer electronics and EVs in SEA.

Passive Components: MLCC Demand Splits; AI and Auto Hold Firm

Murata’s Philippines site lowers exposure to Japanese seismic risk. Samsung Electro-Mechanics is investing in its Philippine MLCC facility to meet automotive demand. Taiyo Yuden is building a $200M dielectric research campus.

📍 Philippines · Status: Investment

Impact: Diversifies MLCC production away from China and Japan; benefits SEA assembly.

Compressors/Motors: Nidec and Kulthorn Active in Thailand

Nidec, which acquired Embraco in 2019, continues to expand its refrigeration compressor business. Kulthorn Kirby in Thailand manufactures reciprocating compressors for refrigerators, freezers, and ACs, using refrigerants including R290 and R600a.

📍 Thailand · Status: Ongoing

Impact: Supports local home appliance manufacturing in Thailand.

Policy Focus

Indonesia: TKDN Regime Expands to AI and Cloud

Indonesia’s local content (TKDN) obligations will extend beyond hardware to AI systems, SaaS platforms, and cloud infrastructure. E-commerce tax policy now designates marketplace platforms as tax collection agents.

Status: Enacted / Phased
Compliance Alert: Electronics importers must verify TKDN certification for government procurement access.

Malaysia: New Tax Incentive Framework and MIDA Expatriate System

Effective June 2026, MIDA’s Expatriate System (MES) is the sole platform for expatriate applications. New tax incentives measure investment commitment against industry averages. SIRIM CMCS introduced new IP Forms for MCMC Type Approval.

Status: Effective June 2026

Singapore: IoT Cybersecurity Labelling Scheme Expands

CSA will raise mandatory cybersecurity requirements for residential routers from CLS Level 1 to Level 2 by 2027. Singapore expanded IoT cybersecurity partnerships with Japan and South Korea for cross-border market access.

Status: Phased

Vietnam: 20% Import Tariff on Electronics; US Framework Signed

Vietnam’s reciprocal tariff rate is set at 20% under the US framework agreement, with potential 0% duty on some articles. The country is pushing innovation to attract next-generation FDI in AI and semiconductors.

Status: Ongoing

SEA Smartphone Shipments Fall 9% in Q1 2026; Malaysia Sees Price Resets

Policy

Malaysia: New MIDA Expatriate System and Tax Incentive Framework Effective June 2026

MIDA’s Expatriate System (MES) is now the sole platform for expatriate applications. Category I minimum salary raised to RM20,000 with a 10-year tenure limit. New tax incentives measure investment commitment against industry averages rather than value-add.

SIRIM CMCS introduced new IP Forms for MCMC Type Approval, replacing trademark authorization letters.

Compliance Alert

Electronics importers must use new IP Forms for Type Approval; expat hiring rules tightened.

Policy

Singapore Raises Cybersecurity Labelling Requirements for Routers to CLS Level 2 by 2027

CSA will work with IMDA to raise mandatory cybersecurity requirements for residential routers from CLS Level 1 to Level 2. The scheme rates IoT devices on security levels. CSA also expanded IoT cybersecurity partnerships with Japan and South Korea.

Cybersecurity licensing framework updates include mandatory certification, extended five-year licence validity, and streamlined notification processes, effective by end-2027.

Compliance Alert

Router and IoT device manufacturers must plan for CLS Level 2 certification by 2027.
